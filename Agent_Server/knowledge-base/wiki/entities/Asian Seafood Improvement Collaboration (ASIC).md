# Asian Seafood Improvement Collaboration (ASIC)

**Description:** A regional collaboration bringing together producers, processors, NGOs, and market stakeholders to improve the sustainability of seafood production in Asia.

**Goals:**
- Develop innovative tools for sustainable production.
- Agree on regional standards.
- Ensure small-scale farms have a voice in sustainability efforts.
