# National Taiwan University

**National Taiwan University (NTU)** is a leading research university in Taiwan.

## Contribution to Fisheries Science
The Institute of Fisheries Science at NTU conducts extensive research on aquaculture, including the genomic and transcriptomic analysis of economically important species like *Macrobrachium rosenbergii*.
