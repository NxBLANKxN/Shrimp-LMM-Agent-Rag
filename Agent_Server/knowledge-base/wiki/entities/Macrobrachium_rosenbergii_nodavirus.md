# Macrobrachium rosenbergii nodavirus

**Macrobrachium rosenbergii nodavirus (MrNV)** is a virus that causes "White Tail Disease" in the giant river prawn (*Macrobrachium rosenbergii*).

## Impact
It is a significant pathogen in aquaculture that can lead to high mortality rates and economic losses.

## Research Application
In transcriptomic studies, samples from MrNV-infected prawns are used to identify differentially expressed genes (DEGs) and understand the host's immune response to the virus.
