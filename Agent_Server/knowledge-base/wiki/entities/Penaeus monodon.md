# Penaeus monodon

**Common Name:** Black Tiger shrimp

**Description:** A species of shrimp native to Southeast Asia, formerly the dominant farmed species until the late 1990s.

**Characteristics:**
- Longer culture time (>100 days).
- Recognized as a premium product due to color, taste, texture, and larger size.
- Performs better in smaller, less intensive farming systems.
- More susceptible to certain viral outbreaks (e.g., WSSV).
