# Yellow Head Virus (YHV)

**Common Name**: 黃頭病毒
**Type**: Virus

## Impact on Shrimp Farming
- **Severity**: A serious viral disease affecting shrimp in Asia.
- **Symptoms**: Characterized by a yellowish discoloration of the cephalothorax.
- **Impact**: Causes rapid mortality in affected ponds.
