# Hyalella azteca

***Hyalella azteca*** is an amphipod crustacean.

## Use in Research
In comparative genomics studies of shrimp, *Hyalella azteca* is often used as an **outgroup** to root phylogenetic trees, helping to determine the evolutionary relationships and divergence times of the target species.
