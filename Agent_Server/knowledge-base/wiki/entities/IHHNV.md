# IHHNV

**Full Name**: Infectious Hypodermal and Hematopoietic Necrosis Virus
**Common Name**: 傳染性皮下與造血組織壞死病毒
**Type**: Virus

## Impact on Shrimp Farming
- **Severity**: Generally considered less lethal than WSSV or TSV, but still significantly impacts growth and survival.
- **Effect**: Causes necrosis in the hypodermis and hematopoietic tissues.
