# Vibrio harveyi

***Vibrio harveyi*** is a pathogenic bacterium that affects various marine and freshwater organisms, including shrimp.

## Impact
It can cause systemic infections in *Macrobrachium rosenbergii*, leading to disease and death in aquaculture settings.

## Research Application
In transcriptomic studies, *V. harveyi* infection is used as a condition to study the expression of immune-related genes and antimicrobial peptides (AMPs) in the host.
