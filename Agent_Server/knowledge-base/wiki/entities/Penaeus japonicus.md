# Penaeus japonicus (斑節蝦)

**Common Name**: Kuruma shrimp
**Type**: Marine Shrimp

## Characteristics
- **Temperature**: Prefers temperate waters; optimal growth temperature is between $25^\circ\text{C}$ and $30^\circ\text{C}$.
- **Salinity**: Optimal growth occurs at salinities of $20\text{--}30\text{‰}$.
- **Economic Value**: Higher market price compared to some other shrimp species, especially as live shrimp.

## Farming Notes
- **Sensitivity**: Highly sensitive to salinity changes and high temperatures in the adult stage.
- **Production**: In intensive systems, partial harvesting can significantly increase yields (e.g., reaching 28 tons/ha in some studies).
