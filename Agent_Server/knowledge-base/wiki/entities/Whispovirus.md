# Whispovirus

**Whispovirus** (often associated with White Spot Syndrome Virus, WSSV) is a virus that causes white spot disease in shrimp.

## Impact
It is one of the most devastating diseases in the shrimp aquaculture industry, causing rapid and widespread mortality.

## Research Application
Samples from Whispovirus-infected *Macrobrachium rosenbergii* are used in transcriptomic analysis to identify genes involved in the viral response and to develop potential disease-resistance markers.
