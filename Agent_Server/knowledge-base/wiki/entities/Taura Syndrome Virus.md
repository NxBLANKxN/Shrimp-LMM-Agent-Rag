# Taura Syndrome Virus (TSV)

**Common Name**: 桃拉病毒
**Type**: Virus

## Impact on Shrimp Farming
- **Severity**: Causes significant losses in shrimp farming, particularly in the Americas.
- **Impact**: Leads to systemic infection and mortality in shrimp.
