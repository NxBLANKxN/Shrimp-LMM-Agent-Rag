# National Taiwan University (國立臺灣大學)

**Type**: Educational Institution
**Location**: Taiwan

## Role in Aquaculture Research
- The **Institute of Fisheries Science** at NTU conducts extensive research on shrimp farming, water quality management, and production enhancement.
- Contributed significant findings on the use of partial harvesting to increase shrimp yields and manage pond carrying capacity.
