# White Spot Syndrome Virus (WSSV)

**Description:** A highly contagious and lethal virus affecting penaeid shrimp.

**Impact:**
- Causes rapid mortality epidemics in shrimp ponds.
- Was a primary driver for the shift from *P. monodon* to *L. vannamei* in the late 1990s.
- Spread through infected stocks, vectors (birds, rodents), and fomites.
- High genetic diversity and adaptability make it difficult to control.
