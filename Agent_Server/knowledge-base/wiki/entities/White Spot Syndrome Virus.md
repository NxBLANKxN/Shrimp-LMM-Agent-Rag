# White Spot Syndrome Virus (WSSV)

**Common Name**: 白斑病毒
**Type**: Virus

## Impact on Shrimp Farming
- **Severity**: One of the most devastating viral diseases in shrimp aquaculture worldwide.
- **Symptoms**: Causes white spots on the carapace and high mortality rates.
- **Transmission**: Highly infectious; can be transmitted via infected seed, water, and other pond organisms (e.g., copepods, barnacles).
- **Management**: There are no effective curative drugs. Prevention focuses on biosecurity, maintaining high water quality (especially high DO), and using SPF (Specific Pathogen Free) seed.
