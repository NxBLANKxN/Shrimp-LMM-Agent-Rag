# FAO (Food and Agriculture Organization)

**Description:** A specialized agency of the United Nations that leads international efforts to defeat hunger and improve nutrition and food security.

**Role in Aquaculture:**
- Provides global production data and reports (e.g., State of World Fisheries and Aquaculture).
- Sets standards and guidelines for sustainable aquaculture practices.
