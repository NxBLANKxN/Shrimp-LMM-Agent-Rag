# Fenneropenaeus chinensis

***Fenneropenaeus chinensis***, known as the Chinese fleshy prawn, is a marine aquaculture species.

## Key Facts
- **Family**: Penaeidae.
- **Genome**: Genome size is approximately 1.58 Gb with 44 pairs of chromosomes.
- **Research**: Studies have identified the role of Simple Sequence Repeats (SSRs) in regulating osmoregulation genes (e.g., *glyA*) under different salinity conditions.
