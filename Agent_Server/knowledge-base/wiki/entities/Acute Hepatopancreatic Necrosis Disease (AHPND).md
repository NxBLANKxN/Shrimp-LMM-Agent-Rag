# Acute Hepatopancreatic Necrosis Disease (AHPND)

**Description:** A devastating bacterial disease in shrimp, often associated with *Vibrio* species.

**Impact:**
- High mortality rates in shrimp aquaculture.
- Linked to poor water quality and lack of biosecurity.
- Causes significant financial losses globally.
