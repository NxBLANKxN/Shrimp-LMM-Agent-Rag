# Best Aquaculture Practices (BAP)

**Description:** An international certification scheme that ensures shrimp and other aquaculture products are produced in a sustainable and safe manner.

**Focus:**
- Environmental responsibility.
- Social accountability.
- Food safety.
- Animal health and welfare.

**Challenges:**
- High cost of certification for smallholders.
- Can create financial dependency on intermediaries.
