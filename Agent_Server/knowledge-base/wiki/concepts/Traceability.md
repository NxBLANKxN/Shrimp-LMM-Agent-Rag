# Traceability (Aquaculture)

**Definition:** The ability to track the movement of an aquaculture product through all stages of production, processing, and distribution, from the farm to the final consumer.

**Technological Enablers:**
- **Blockchain**: Used to create immutable records of origin, health certificates, and handling.
- **AI and IoT**: Sensors and data analytics to monitor pond conditions and health in real-time.
- **Satellite Technology**: Used for monitoring land use and environmental impact.

**Importance:**
- Reduces food fraud and spoilage.
- Ensures food safety and compliance with international standards.
- Builds consumer confidence by providing transparency on animal welfare and biodiversity impacts.
