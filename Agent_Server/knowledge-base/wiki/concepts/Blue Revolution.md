# Blue Revolution

**Definition:** The industrialization of aquaculture since the 1960s, aiming to provide a sustainable solution to global food security by scaling up the production of aquatic animals, plants, and algae.

**Impact:**
- Led to the rapid growth of the aquaculture sector.
- Increased the availability of protein-rich "Blue Foods".
- Introduced intensive farming techniques that increased yield but also brought new challenges related to disease and environmental impact.
