# Blue Foods

**Definition:** Foods obtained from oceans, lakes, and rivers, including both wild-caught and cultivated (aquaculture) species. This includes fish, shrimp, algae, and other aquatic animals.

**Key Characteristics:**
- Essential for global diets and economies.
- Provide nearly 20% of global animal protein.
- Critical sources of micronutrients (e.g., Vitamin B12) and essential fatty acids (Omega-3).
- Often have a lower environmental cost compared to terrestrial animal proteins due to better Feed Conversion Ratios (FCR).
