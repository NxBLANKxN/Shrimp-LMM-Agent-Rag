# Biosecurity (Aquaculture)

**Definition:** A set of preventative measures designed to reduce the risk of introduction and spread of pathogens (viruses, bacteria, parasites) within and between aquaculture farms.

**Common Measures:**
- Controlled access to farms and foot dipping.
- Use of disease-resistant strains and certified disease-free post-larvae (PL).
- Installation of nets to prevent avian or rodent vectors from entering ponds.
- Regular disinfection and pond cleaning between crops.
- Use of prebiotics to boost innate immunity.
