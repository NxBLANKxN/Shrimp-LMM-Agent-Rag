# Aquamimicry

**Definition:** An aquaculture design approach that mimics natural aquatic ecosystems to manage nutrient cycles and waste, often by integrating different species or habitats to create a balanced environment.

**Application in Shrimp Farming:**
- Use of additional ponds for other species to handle nitrogenous waste.
- Diversification of production to create a more resilient system.
- Often paired with Biofloc technology to reduce water consumption.
